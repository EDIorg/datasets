Micro-climatic data for solar radiation and temperature recorded in Panoche Hills Managament Area in Califronia, U.S.A. The study was conducted Spring-Summer 2019 (May 20th- June 12th). Micro-climate was recorded using in situ loggers under the shrubs Ephedra californica, artificial shelters, and the open to test for differences. Macro-site level data was retrieved from the nearby weather station and all data were compiled into one dataset.
