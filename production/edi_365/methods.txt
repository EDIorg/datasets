In July 2015, we collected a surface peat core (10 cm diam.) from each plot and divided it into 0-5 and 5-10 cm depth increments. Samples were frozen and shipped to Ward Laboratories, Kearney, Nebraska, for quantification of phospholipid fatty acid (PLFA) profiles (cf. Andersen et al. 2010).
