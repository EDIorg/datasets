A handheld multiparameter sonde took measurements at 98 points across a 65 meter grid across the Swan Lake at 0.25 meter below the surface. Measurements were made weekly, with the exception of the last two sampling events that were two weeks apart. Sonde was equipped with sensors measuring temperature, dissolved oxygen, pH, chlorophyll a, phycocyanin, conductivity, and turbidity. Measurements were taken between 9:30 and 17:00 for all 17 sampling events. The handheld multiparameter sonde was YSI ProDSS (Xylem, Inc), all probes were calibrated weekly.
