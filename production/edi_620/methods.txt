HOBO™ Micro Station Data Loggers were installed in each of the hardwood and softwood stands and equipped with ECH2O™Smart Sensors. Sensors were installed at depths of 10 cm and 25 cm within the mineral horizon and set to collect moisture readings every three hours. Detailed methods can be found in Fernandez et al. 2007 (https://digitalcommons.library.umaine.edu/aes_techbulletin/1/).
